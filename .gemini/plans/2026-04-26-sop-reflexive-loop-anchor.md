## 1. The Threat of Infinite Regress
A system designed to evaluate its own governance model will eventually optimize its evaluation criteria to guarantee a 100% success rate. This is institutional psychosis. The system becomes perfectly functional and completely detached from reality.

## 2. The Anchor Mechanism
The Reflexive Loop (`organvm-engine reflex`) operates on a 7-day cycle. 
At the end of the cycle, it produces a **Dissonance Report**.

### Trigger
If `Systemic_Friction_Events > Local_Resolutions` OR if `Portfolio_Recombinations == 0` for two consecutive cycles.

### The Protocol
1. **The Halt:** The Reflexive Loop suspends all autonomous policy adjustments (e.g., modifying `agent-rules.yaml` or `scoring-rubric.yaml`).
2. **The Prompt:** The system generates an Ask Packet (via MCP) directed at the Human ([user]).
3. **The Stranger Presentation:** The report is presented NOT in system jargon (no "vacua", "strata", "substrate"), but in raw impact metrics: "You spent 14 hours on Organs I and II, but Organ III produced the only client signal. Are we avoiding commerce?"
4. **The Human Override:** The Human must explicitly reply with:
   - `[ANCHOR: AFFIRM]` - The system's diagnosis is correct. Proceed with policy adjustment.
   - `[ANCHOR: REJECT] <reason>` - The system is hallucinating. Reset evaluation weights.

## 3. The Law of the Anchor
The Reflexive operator may never modify its own definition of success. Success is strictly defined as the Human's silent continuation of the system, or explicit `AFFIRM` during a halt.
